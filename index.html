Crystals Collector!

You will be given a random number at the start of the game.

There are four crystals below. By clicking on a crystal you will add a specific amout of points to you total score.

You win the game by watching your total score to random number, you lose the game if your total score goes above the random number.

The value of each crystal is hiddne from you until you click on it.

Each time when the game starts, the game will change the values of each crystal.
